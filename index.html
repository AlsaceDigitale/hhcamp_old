HACKING HEALTH

CAMP

Strasbourg

March 26th to 29th 2015

Be informed when registration opens

Hacking Health Camp is a global event for all people interested in health innovation, inspiring health professionals, designers, developers, UX researchers and engineers about their common amazing possibilities in tomorrow’s health.

Learning, inspiration and action in 4 days with 4 events


HEALTH
DATA DAY

HEALTH HACKATHON

Thursday, march 26th 9h-17h

"There was more Health data created in the first quarter of 2014, then in all of recorded history" (source). One full day of workshops and conferences gravitating around the technical, medical, privacy and legal aspects of producing and consuming health data. Big Data in healthcare is here, how we can use it ? Learn more...

Thursday, march 26th 19h

One evening party dedicated to health startups with the presence of investors. Early-stage startups will pitch; attendees will vote by investing in their favorite startups with fake banknotes. The winner will be the one who raises the most money. Learn more...

Friday, march 27th 9h-17h

One day of insipiring and forward-thinking conferences on the future of health by a line up of internationally-recognized health mentors. Learn more...

Friday march 27th 17h to Sunday, march 29th 20h

The second annual hackathon will run from Friday evening to Sunday evening. Health professionals, developers and designers will create prototypes based on challenges submitted before the event. Learn more...
